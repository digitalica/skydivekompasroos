Skydive Kompasroos 


is a Android app to help Dutch skydivers to choose the correct canopy, according to the rules set by the safety regulations of the skydive department of the KNVvL (Royal Dutch Aviation Accociation)

The safety regulations and more information:
http://www.parachute.nl/bevoegdhedenregl.html

The 'kompasroos' itself:
http://www.parachute.nl/fileadmin/knvvlpa_upload/pdf/Kompasroos.pdf

Safety regulations addition A: Rules for choosing canopies, version 2011
http://www.parachute.nl/fileadmin/knvvlpa_upload/pdf/BVR_Bijlage_A_2011.pdf

Safety regulstions addition B: Canopy categories, version 2011-07 (july)
http://www.parachute.nl/fileadmin/knvvlpa_upload/pdf/BVR_bijlage_B_versie_2011-07_juli.pdf

We plan to publish this app under the GNU Public Licens. The source can be found at GitHub already:
https://github.com/digitalica/skydivekompasroos

The app is in Google Play:
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=nl.digitalica.skydivekompasroos

Beware: the app is in testing fase! No rights can be derived.
