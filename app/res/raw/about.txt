Skydive Kompasroos 

is an Android app to help Dutch skydivers to choose the correct canopy, according to the rules set by the Basic Safety Regulations of the skydive department of the KNVvL (Royal Netherlands Aeronautical Association, the National Aviation Administration)

Always check with an instructor. He or she will be responsible. No rights can be derived from this app. The official KNVvL documentation is:

The safety regulations and more information:
http://www.parachute.nl/bevoegdhedenregl.html

The 'kompasroos' itself:
http://www.parachute.nl/fileadmin/knvvlpa_upload/pdf/Kompasroos.pdf

Safety regulations addition A, rules for choosing canopies, version 2011:
http://www.parachute.nl/fileadmin/knvvlpa_upload/pdf/BVR_Bijlage_A_2011.pdf

Safety regulations addition B, canopy categories, version maart 2013:
http://www.parachute.nl/fileadmin/knvvlpa_upload/pdf/BVR_bijlage_B_versie_2013_maart.pdf
